Scope

What it can cover

A California divorce settlement addresses parenting plans, child support, spousal support, and division of assets and debts. We start with what matters most to you and build from there.

If your family has layers — mental health, sobriety, domestic violence, a child with different needs — that is not a reason to avoid this process. It is a reason to find the right mediator.
